ENEL 361

Electronic Devices and Materials

A screenshot from an oscillicope reading during a photoresistor lab.

In ENEL 361, we learned about electronic devices, specifially those that used semiconductors such as diodes, light-emitting diodes, photodies, and transistors. I liked this course because I loved learning about the materials that make up our everyday products like phones, laptops, and much more. The knowledge we learned about devices like diodes and transistors open up lots of new possibilities for us to create our own circuits with.
