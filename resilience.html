Resilience

What is Resilience?

Over the course of my education I’ve developed a resilient mindset. Resilience allows me to understand and deal with negative emotions, helping me rebound from the setbacks that will inevitably happen.

One way I've incoporated resilience into my life is to adapt a growth mindset rather than a fixed mindset; I believe in my own abilities and continue to strive to be the best person I can be.

My Mindset

I believe that failure is simply an avenue to promote further growth in me. Of course, nobody likes to fail, but it’s important to control our emotions and not let negative thoughts get to us. This type of mindset allows me to remain calm when things go wrong.

Being able to stand strong in the face of adversity is something I’m proud of. I’m also happy to bring this positive energy with me for my peers and become a leader that lifts them up.
