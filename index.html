Who am I?

Student at the University of Calgary

I'm a second year electrical an digital engineering student at the University of Calgary. For most of my life I've been passionate about science and technology, and hope to help drive change in the world using them. I enjoy coding and am experienced with C, C++, Python, HTML, and CSS. Currently, I'm helping a team develop a social media app for the Engineering Students' Society.

So far during my short time at the university, I've been able to meet fantastic people who share my passion for science and represent my fellow students as the Professional Development Commissioner on the Engineering Students' Society. Recently, I've taken a role on the Schulich Ignite team as the Internal Communications Commissioner, and hope to imbue the same passion I hold in the engineers and computer scientists of the future.
